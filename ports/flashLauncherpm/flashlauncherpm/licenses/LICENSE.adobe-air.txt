Adobe AIR Runtime (bundled as captive runtime)
Maintained by Harman International (https://airsdk.harman.com/)

The Adobe AIR runtime is included as a captive runtime bundle as part of
the FlashLauncherZJ application (https://github.com/StarCatL/FlashLauncherZJ).

Adobe AIR is proprietary software. Redistribution is permitted under
Harman's captive runtime licensing model, which allows developers to
bundle the AIR runtime with their applications.
